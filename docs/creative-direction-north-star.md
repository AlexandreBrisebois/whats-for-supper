# Creative Direction: The North Star

This document captures the soul and strategy of the "What's for Supper" project. It serves as the ultimate design benchmark for all future features, refinements, and expansions.

## 1. Vision Statement
To transform the daily stress of meal planning into a **calm, collaborative, and beautiful sanctuary**. We are not building a spreadsheet for recipes; we are building the "Hearth" of the digital home.

---

## 2. Design Philosophy: The Mère-Designer
All design decisions are filtered through the "Busy Parent" reality:
*   **Sanity-First UX**: Every tap must reduce anxiety, not increase it.
*   **The Toddler Rule**: If it can't be operated with one thumb while holding a child, it's not finished.
*   **Speed over Specs**: Native OS behaviors (like the camera) are preferred over custom UI "middlemen" to ensure zero friction.

---

## 3. Visual Identity: Solar Earth
Grounded in nature, energy, and warmth.

*   **Color Palette**:
    *   **Terracotta (#CD5D45)**: The hearth, grounding, and decisive. Used for "Veto" and primary actions.
    *   **Ochre (#E1AD01)**: Solar energy and optimism. Used for highlights and "Eureka" moments.
    *   **Sage Green (#8A9A5B)**: Freshness and calm. Used for "Matches," "Likes," and Produce sections.
    *   **Soft Cream (#FDFCF0)**: The canvas. A warm, non-clinical background.
*   **Typography**:
    *   **Outfit**: Editorial headings with character.
    *   **Inter**: High-legibility UI sans for data-heavy views.
*   **Visual Language**:
    *   **Glassmorphism**: Translucent panels with 12px blur to create depth and focus.
    *   **Organic Shapes**: Flowing, soft-cornered backgrounds to contrast with the "grid" of daily life.
    *   **Macro Photography**: Food is the hero—vibrant, high-saturation, and oversized.

---

## 4. The Core Pillars (The "North Star" Features)

### A. The Command Center (Home)
The Home screen is not a dashboard; it is an active hub. It answers "What's for Supper?" in 2 seconds and manages "Supper Prep" in 10.

### B. Matchmaking Discovery (Social)
Discovery is a "team sport." We use high-engagement card stacks to build family consensus. A "match" is more than a like—it's a household decision.

### C. Zero-Friction Capture (Acquisition)
Capture is the top of the funnel. It must be invisible. No custom viewfinders—just the power of the native device camera to get the data into the AI pipeline as fast as possible.

### D. The Efficient Grocer (Utility)
Organization that respects the store layout. We group by section (Produce → Meat → Dairy) to ensure the user never has to double back.

---

## 5. Future Horizons
*   **Cook's Mode**: High-visibility "at-the-stove" UI.
*   **Identity Pulse**: Subtle UI shifts to reflect who is currently "driving" the kitchen.
*   **Offline Resilience**: A beautiful "No-Connection" experience that maintains household calm.
